5-4-3-2-1 grounding

  • 5Name 5 things you can see around you right now.
  • 4Notice 4 things you can physically feel — feet on the floor, air on your skin.
  • 3Identify 3 things you can hear — traffic, birdsong, your own breath.
  • 2Find 2 things you can smell, or two scents you enjoy.
  • 1Notice 1 thing you can taste, or one flavor you love.
